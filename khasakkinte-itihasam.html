Khasakkinte Itihasam

Author: O. V. Vijayan

Genre: Magical realism

Publication date: 1969

“Ravi, a disillusioned young man who retreats to the remote village of Khasak to escape his past and inner conflicts. As he becomes a teacher in a local one-room school, Ravi is drawn into the unique blend of folklore, myths, and spirituality that define the village’s essence. The novel explores his interactions with the villagers, each of whom carries their own mystic stories and beliefs, blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. Ravi’s journey is marked by an existential search for meaning as he confronts his guilt, desires, and the weight of his decisions. Ultimately, the novel offers a meditation on human isolation, the passage of time, and the elusive nature of truth.”
