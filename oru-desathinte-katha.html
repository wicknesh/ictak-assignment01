Oru Desathinte Katha

Author: S. K. Pottekkatt

Genre: Historical fiction, Social fiction

Publication date: 1971

“A novel that delves into the social and political dynamics of a fictional village in Kerala. The story revolves around the experiences of the village's inhabitants as they face the impact of modernization and political upheaval. Through the lives of various characters, the novel explores themes of social justice, change, and the clash between tradition and progress. It vividly portrays the struggles of the common people and the complexities of their interactions with the changing world. The novel is a poignant reflection on the transformative effects of socio-political forces on rural life.”
