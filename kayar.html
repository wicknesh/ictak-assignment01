Kayar

Author: Thakazhi Sivasankara Pillai

Genre: Historical fiction

Publication date: 1984

“A sweeping epic that spans several decades, depicting the socio-economic transformations of a village in Kerala. The novel follows the lives of its characters against the backdrop of the changing landscape of rural life, exploring their struggles, ambitions, and interactions. Central to the story is the character of Kunjukunju, whose personal journey reflects the broader societal shifts and the impact of modernization on traditional ways of life. As the village evolves, so do the relationships and conflicts among its inhabitants, highlighting themes of love, power, and resistance. Kayar offers a rich and intricate portrayal of rural Kerala, capturing the essence of its cultural and historical milieu.”
