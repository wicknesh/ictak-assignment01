Aarachaar

Author: K. R. Meera

Genre: Historical drama, psychological drama

Publication date: December, 2012

“The story of Chetna Grddha Mullick, the last in a long line of executioners, who becomes the first woman to take up the mantle of her ancestral profession in modern-day Bengal. As Chetna steps into this role, she grapples with the weight of her family’s legacy, societal judgment, and her own moral dilemmas surrounding life and death. The novel explores her complex inner world as she faces the paradox of seeking justice through the act of execution while struggling with the deeply ingrained patriarchal structures around her. Chetna’s interactions with the condemned prisoners, her family, and the society at large expose her vulnerability and strength in equal measure. At its core, Aarachar is a profound meditation on power, gender, tradition, and the inescapable burdens of one’s heritage.”
